About this mithqál calculator

This calculator is made to convert between Bahá'í mithqáls of gold or silver and various world currencies.

It supports around 40 ISO 4217 certified currencies leveraging real-time exchange rate APIs for both the metals and the currencies using the USD as the base currency. The live APIs insure up to date and accurate conversions.

The CUSTOM rate allows you to use your real world accurate currency rate in relation to the US Dollar. So for example, if one US dollar equates to 50 of your currency input 50 in the box. If one US dollar equates to 0.85 of your currency input 0.85. Use This if your official currency rate may not be accurate or you would rather have your rates on a set standard.
